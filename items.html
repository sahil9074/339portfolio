The Culture in Sikhism

Gatka, a martial art that is associated with stick fighting or even with real swords and shields.

Chakkar, a steel ring that was used as a weapon by the Sikh military. This was either by throwing it or spinning it around.

The Five Ks of Sikhism are five items of faith that mark a Sikh's identity and symbolises their dedication to God.

Harmonium, an instrument that is typically used in all Sikh musical worship performances (Kirtan).

Tabla, an instrument that accompanies the harmonium as a drum.

A demonstration of a dhol, a double-headed drum that accompanies folk music.
