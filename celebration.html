Sikh Celebrations and Events

Bandi Chhor Divas, a celebration for when our sixth Guru, Guru Hargobind released 52 prisoners without any fighting.

Vaisakhi, the celebration of the birth of the Khalsa as well as the spring harvest in India.

Gurpurab celebrates the birth of the first Sikh Guru, Guru Nanak Dev Ji.

Operation Bluestar, a military operation carried out by Indian forces on the holiest site for Sikhs, the Golden Temple.

Holding protests becasue the Government of India has not owned up to their actions nor prosectued nearly enough people.

The 1984 Sikh Genocide, a 4 day massacre that led to many deaths and many Sikhs fleeing India.

A protest held in San Francisco to spread awareness about the Farmer's Protest in India

Three laws were passed that Sikhs, who are the majority of farmers did not agree with because it would hurt their livelihood. This protest lasted for a year until the laws were finally revocated.

A protest held in New Delhi, India to spread awareness about the Farmer's Protest and our rights.
